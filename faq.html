Frequently Asked Questions



So I already have a listing on a big letting portal – how will this work?

Sites created by Your Holiday Home Online aim to be an accompaniment to any existing listings. We understand the market is very cluttered and it’s hard to create standout – this is a great way to do so.


I don’t have any written text for the listing! Help!

We have a professional copy writer who can create content for you. This is priced at £100 per 500 words.


How much flexibility do I get on your design?

The sites are priced to reflect the design, but we are happy to consider changes that won’t require a lot of work.


Any chance of a discount for multiple properties?

Yes we will be happy to negotiate a discount if you want more than one website built.


What does the web hosting bit mean?

That’s how the website is delivered to the internet. We manage that for the fee specified.


So once the site is built, what do i do with it then?

You’ll have your own site with it’s own address, there’s a number of ways you can boost your marketing efforts including social media, paying to appear at the top of Google search rankings etc. We can help with this, please ask.
